Getting started with myLite

To use myLite, kindly follow this simple steps:
  • enter www.myLite.ng in the search engine of any of the supported browsers
    1. Checking for power
    2. This should bring you to our Home page. Enter your address in the search box and select your Neighbourhood from the dropdown. Click the search button and this should get your power status. From there you can continue to the rest of site

TroubleShooting

Accessing yuur location might be one of those problems that can arise. Follow this simple steps to reset your location settings. (Video demo to come soon)

  • Browser Location settings

    myLite is supported on all major browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, IE8 and above. To manipulate the location services for the following, you can:

    • For Google Chrome :
      1. In the browser navigation button, click SETTINGS
      2. go to ADVANCED SETTINGS > PRIVACY > CONTENT SETTINGS
      3. scroll to LOCATIONS and enable as appropriate
      4. return to the site and allow us get your location. Please be sure that Javascript is enabled on your device(s)
    • For Safari
      1. click the SETTINGS dropdown at the far right corner of the browser Toolbar
      2. Go to PREFERENCES and then to PRIVACY
      3. enable Location services by choosing the appropriate option. Please be sure that Javascript is enabled on your device(s)
    • For Mozilla Firefox
      1. click the Menu button button at the far right corner of the browser Toolbar
      2. select privacy from the navigation menu bar at the side
